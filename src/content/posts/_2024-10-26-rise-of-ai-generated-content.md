## Summary
An analysis of the impact of AI-generated content on the web, from SEO and content marketing to the spread of misinformation. This content is for web developers, content creators, and marketers who are grappling with the opportunities and challenges of AI-generated content.

## Why now
The proliferation of AI-generated content is forcing a re-evaluation of how we create, consume, and rank content on the web. As search engines and social media platforms adapt their algorithms to handle AI-generated content, it is crucial for creators and developers to understand the implications for their work.
