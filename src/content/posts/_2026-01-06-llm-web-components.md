## Summary

A practical guide on how to use large language models (LLMs) to generate accessible, ARIA-compliant web components. This post will cover the benefits of using LLMs for this task, as well as the potential pitfalls to avoid.

## Why now

Web accessibility is a critical concern, and developers are always looking for ways to build more inclusive web applications. The use of LLMs to automate the generation of accessible components is a new and exciting area of research that has the potential to save developers a significant amount of time and effort.
