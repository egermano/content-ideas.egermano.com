## Summary

A tutorial on building a blog that can answer questions about its own content, using a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipeline to provide contextually relevant answers.

## Why now

RAG is a powerful technique for making LLMs more accurate and knowledgeable about specific domains. It's a great way to add a unique and useful feature to a personal blog or documentation site.
