## Experiment plans

### Experiment 1 — Build a "summarize this article" application

- Objective: To build a full-stack application that can summarize any article on the web.
- Stack: Next.js, the Vercel AI SDK, and an LLM API.
- Steps:
  1. Create a Next.js application with a simple UI for inputting a URL.
  2. Use the Vercel AI SDK to stream a summary of the article from an LLM.
  3. Display the summary to the user.
- Expected: The application can accurately summarize any article on the web.
- Time est: 8–10 hours
- Difficulty: high
- Safety/privacy/licensing: Be mindful of the terms of service of the websites you are summarizing.
