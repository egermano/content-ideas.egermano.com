### Experiment 1 — Convert a complex Figma design with Locofy
- Objective: To test the accuracy and efficiency of Locofy by converting a complex, multi-component Figma design.
- Stack: Figma account, Locofy account, React/Vue project.
- Steps:
  1. Create a complex design in Figma that includes nested components, auto layout, and variants.
  2. Use the Locofy plugin to convert the design into code.
  3. Manually review and refactor the generated code to meet production standards.
- Expected: A functional, pixel-perfect implementation of the Figma design.
